I am currently in my third year as a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Quantum Optics, Ulm University, under the guidance of Professor Fedor Jelezko. My research focuses on solid-state systems for quantum information, specializing in high-precision measurements. My work extends to diverse applications in the fields of biology and material science, exploring the potential impact of quantum technologies on these areas.
